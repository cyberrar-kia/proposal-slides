How I Work With Feedback

On revisions
& detail

You described yourselves as thorough reviewers. That's exactly what I need from a client — vague feedback produces vague results.

📋
Written feedback → methodical implementation
I don't skim revision notes. I go line by line, implement each item, and re-deploy. If something isn't addressed, you'll see it in the re-deployment — not in an excuse.
📱
Mobile-first, not mobile-adapted
Every component starts at 375px. I test on real devices before submitting for review. I won't ask you to review a milestone I haven't tested myself on mobile.
⚡
Fast turnaround on revision rounds
Within your 48-hour feedback window, I implement and re-deploy the same day. Your review rhythm shouldn't be held up by mine.

Real proof: My most recent project involved 70+ detailed revision items across a 9-page site (fasttrackliteracy.com). Every item was delivered. The client called it "the last set of changes." That's the standard I work to.
